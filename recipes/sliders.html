Description

Small in size, big in flavor. These sliders are made to impress — whether you’re grilling, snacking, or hosting.

Juicy fillings, soft buns, and bold flavors in every bite. Perfect for parties, game days, or a fun family dinner, sliders bring a playful twist to classic comfort food.

Mix and match different meats, sauces, and toppings to create your own flavor combos, or stick to the timeless favorites that never disappoint.

Easy to make, quick to serve, and guaranteed to disappear fast — these sliders are the ultimate crowd-pleasers.

Ingredient List

  • Hawaiann Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Ham/Turkey
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Slice bread in half
  2. Put ham and cheese in between bread
  3. Put your sandwhiches onto banking pan
  4. Butter up the buns of the sandwhich with melted butter
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 min
